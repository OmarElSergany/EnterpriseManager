Where ever you see lists within the POS you can change the order of the columns, resize the columns and sort ascending/descending on any column.  All settings will be remembered.

Sort Columns               -        Click the column header, the first click will sort the column ascending, subsequent clicks will alternate the sorting between descending and ascending.

Resize Lists                  -        Move the mouse between the column headers, the cursor will change to a line with an arrow pointing both left and right. Hold the left mouse button down and drag the column to the desired size.

Auto Resize Column -    Move the cursor between the column headers until it changes (as above) and double click, the column will be resized to the most appropriate size for the content.

Change Column Order -        To change the column order, place your mouse anywhere in the column, hold down the left mouse button and move your mouse left or right.  When the column is in the desired position, release the left mouse button.

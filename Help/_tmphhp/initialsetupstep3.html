Step 3 of the initial setup is used to enter your logo, this can be completed at a later time and is used for letter heads, invoices, labels and on your website (if integrated).

To select your logo, double click and select the logo file, or drag the logo file over the white space and drop it.

Once selected your logo will be shown below.


The logo must be at least  178 pixels wide by 121 pixels high.

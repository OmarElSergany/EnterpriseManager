Split Payments
 Previous Top Next
Split payment allows customers to pay using several different methods, including vouchers.

Amount Due             -             Total amount to be paid by the customer.

Total Tendered       -             The amount of payment currently tendered by the customer.

Balance                    -             The amount still due from the customer, if the customer has tendered an amount over what is due, it will appear red and have a minus number.  This is the amount of change that should be given back.

Total Cash              -             Total cash value tendered by the customer.

Total Cheque          -             Total cheque value tendered by the customer.

Total Card              -             Total card value tendered by the customer.

Total Voucher11 Please see company policy on whether a voucher can be partially redeemed.       -             Total voucher value tendered by the customer.
Click OK where you will be prompted to print an invoice for the customer.

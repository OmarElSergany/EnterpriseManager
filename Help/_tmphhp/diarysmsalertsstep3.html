During step 3, the final step, messages will be sent to customers.  The wizard will tell you how many messages will be sent.  To send the reminder alters click send.

A reminder alert can only be sent once, for each appointment.  Once a message has been sent you cannot send another.
You can check whether a notification has been sent or not by hovering over the appointment within the diary, it will either say “Reminder sent” or “Reminder not sent”.

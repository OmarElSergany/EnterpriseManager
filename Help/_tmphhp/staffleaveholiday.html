Stores Leave/Holiday entitlement and requests for staff members.


Entitlement                -           Staff members leave entitlement.

Days                            -           Signifies that the entitlement is in days.

Hours                          -           Signifies that the entitlement is in hours.

Carries Over              -           If ticked, any unused leave can be carried over to the next leave year, if unused.

Accrues                      -           If ticked, the leave entitlement is accrued in line with the staff members working hours.

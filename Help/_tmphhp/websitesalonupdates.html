Users who are assigned to salons can update the salon details online on your website, themselves, this includes adding images.  Each salon update will appear in the list and has to be approved prior to being updated on the website.  This provides users with an opportunity to verify the details being given.
Double click the update to view the differences.
Once the differences have been approved or denied the update will be removed from the form.

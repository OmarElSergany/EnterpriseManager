You can view all customer details in the POS.

You can update the customer basic details.

Clean Address         -             Attempts to remove duplicate entries from the address.

Send Password         -             Sends an email to the customer with a reminder of their password.

Reset Password        -             If for some reason the customer is not receiving emails, this option will generate a random password, you can inform the customer of the password so they can login.

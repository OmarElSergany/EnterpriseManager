Throughout the POS, there are a number of standard buttons displayed on many of the forms shown, these are designed to indicate actions available across a number of areas.  The buttons are always displayed on the top of each form.

If the button appears “greyed out” it is disabled and cannot be used.  When the mouse is hovered over each button a hint will appear indicating what action the button will perform.

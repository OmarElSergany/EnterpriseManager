Entering Payment
 Previous Top Next
After clicking OK you will need to enter the payment details.
Select the payment type to be used, you can select from:

Cash                         -             Used when the sale is cash only.

Card                         -             Used when the sale is credit/debit card only.

Cheque                     -             Used when cheque payment is made.

Split Payment          -             Used when a combination of payment types will be used.
When making a payment via Card/Cheque you will be provided with a section to make notes, if required.

Stock In
 Previous Top Next
If you need to move stock into the system, you can specify the quantities in Stock In.

Specify the quantity of stock being moved in for each stock item.  You can filter the products using the filter.
When complete click the X in the top right corner.
Only stock with a “Quantity In” above 0 (zero) will be moved into stock.
Stock being moved out will be logged against the user moving stock out.  See Stock History for more details.

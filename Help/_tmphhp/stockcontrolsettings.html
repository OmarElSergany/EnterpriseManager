Stock Control Settings
 Previous Top Next
These settings affect the display of the Stock Control form.

Show Stock Low      -             The value at which stock will appear low and be set to the colour defined below.

Stock Low Selected              -  The colour of a stock item when it is selected and the stock is low, i.e. in the example above, if the minimum level was set to 3 and the stock availability was 10, then it would be this colour when selected.

Stock Low               -             As above, except the stock item is not selected by the user.

Stock Warning        -             Colour used to indicate the quantity of stock is becoming low.

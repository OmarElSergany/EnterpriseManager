The treatments tab indicates what treatments a member of staff is able to complete, this allows the diary booking system to only show/select what treatments are assigned.

To add an existing treatment, either highlight it in the Available Treatments list and click “Add”, or double click the item within the Available Treatments.
To remove a treatment from the assigned treatment list, either double click the treatment to be removed, or highlight it and click “Remove”.

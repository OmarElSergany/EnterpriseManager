Discount Coupons
 Previous Top Next
Discount coupons can be used by the Till module and on the website.  There is no limit to the number of active coupons.
The name of the coupon must be unique and is not case sensitive.

You can choose to view active coupon codes or all codes within the database.
Double click an existing coupon to edit, or click “New” to create a new coupon.
When you edit or create a coupon you will be shown a Wizard interface to guide you through the steps of setting up your coupon.
See the Apply Discount section to see how the coupon can be redeemed in the Till

To administer staff members you can select Staff menu and then click Manage Staff, you will be presented with the following form:

Toolbar Icons
The following list describes what the toolbar icons represent:

               Add new staff member.

              Remove selected staff member.  This includes checks to make sure the staff member can be deleted, if not, a notification will appear.

               Save changes the selected staff member.

               Refreshes the staff list.
The left part of the form shows the staff in a hierarchical representation.  Clicking on the staff member will show you their details on the right hand side.
To move a member of staff select the staff member, drag the staff member by holding the left mouse button down, drag the staff member over the member of staff who they will report to and release the left mouse button.

Information on expense claims can be viewed by staff members submitting the expense, or staff members that have permission to Manage Expenses.


The items can be filtered by user and by status type by selecting the correct option from the available lists.

There are four status values, they are:

·    Submitted    -    Initially entered by a staff member.
·    Approved     -    Expense has been approved.
·    Declined -    The expense claim has been declined.
·    Paid   -    The expense claim has been marked as paid for the staff members pay check.

If an expense claim has been refused, the member of staff submitting it can edit the claim to make any necessary changes.

Right clicking an expense item will load a menu allowing staff members to take appropriate action, as well as viewing a receipt.

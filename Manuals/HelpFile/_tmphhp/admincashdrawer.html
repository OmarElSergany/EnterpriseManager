The POS includes a module for users to verify Cash held in remote sites.
There are three options available, all from selecting the Accounts menu then the cash menu, they are:

Till                          -             Provides options for users to audit cash kept in a till.

Safe                          -             Provides options for users to audit cash kept in a safe.

Petty Cash               -             Provides options for users to audit petty cash.
All transactions taking place within the Till, Safe or Petty Cash are logged with the current logged in user.

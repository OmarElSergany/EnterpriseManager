You can view and respond to tickets within the POS.
To change the department or status change them using the options and click close.  To reply to the ticket, enter your response and click Reply.  Clicking the X in the top left will close the form without making any changes.

KEY                         -             Ticket Reference Number.

Subject                     -             Ticket subject as defined by the customer.

Created By              -             Customer that created the ticket.

Last Replier            -              Person who last responded on the ticket.

Last Updated           -             Date and time of the last response to the ticket.

Department              -             The department who the ticket is for.

Priority                   -             The priority given to the ticket by the customer.

Status                      -             Current status of the ticket.

Find User                 -             Finds the user account associated with the ticket, if they have one.

Print                        -             Prints the ticket in full.

Reply                        -             Enter your reply in the text area provided and hit the Reply button.  The ticket will close and be placed in the On Hold section.

Close                       -             Closes viewing of the ticket.  The status and department selected when viewing will determine the ticket status upon closing.

Campaign Name        -             Enter the campaign name during this step.  The campaign name can only be 16 characters long and must only include the following characters: A to Z, a to z, 0 to 9 and - (hyphen).

Website URL           -             The website URL indicates the website where traffic will be redirected to.

Load Campaign         -              If you have previously saved a campaign you can load it by clicking Load Campaign and selecting there relevant file.

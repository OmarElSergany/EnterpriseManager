The employment details for users are held within this section.

Job title                     -           Job title for staff member.

Location                     -           Location where staff member works from.

Part Time                   -           Indicates the staff member is a part time, if ticked.  If left unticked the staff member is considered full time.

Weekly Hours            -           The standard number of working hours for a staff member.

Payroll Number         -           Employees payroll number.

Pay Period                  -           Frequency of pay.

Employment Type       -           Type of employment.

Dates                          -           Date of joining/when permanent employment starts if a probation period is defined.

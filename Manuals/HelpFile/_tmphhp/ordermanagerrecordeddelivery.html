This option can be used to provide a unique reference for recorded deliver.


Local/International              -  Two identical sections for local and international settings.

Prefix                       -             Text to prefix the unique number.

Unique Number         -             The starting unique number.

Suffix                       -             The text appearing after the unique number.

Auto Increment       -             Determines whether the number is automatically incremented after use.
